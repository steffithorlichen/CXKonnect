About

Stefanie Thorlichen

From pediatric nursing in Germany to leading global CX transformation at scale, I have spent 30 years learning one thing: the most important thing you can do for another person is make them feel genuinely seen.

I have built, led, and redesigned customer experience organizations across telecommunications, technology, healthcare, and professional services — spanning 30 countries, thousands of team members, and millions of customer relationships.

CX Konnect is the product of everything I have learned about what actually works: not feelings-first CX, but outcome-driven systems where every team, every metric, and every AI investment is aligned around the customer’s success.

The Methodology

The CX Konnect Framework

Customers do not experience your org chart. They experience the gaps in it.
🎯
Outcome-Driven
Shift from measuring activity and sentiment to measuring whether customers achieve the results they signed up for. Every metric, milestone, and handoff is anchored to a tangible customer outcome.
⚙️
Cross-Functionally Connected
Break the silos between Sales, Support, Success, Product, Education, and Partners. Build shared accountability structures where every team understands its role in the customer’s journey.
🧠
AI-Amplified
Deploy AI as the intelligence layer, not just a tool. Health scoring, predictive signals, automated playbooks, and conversation intelligence working as a connected system alongside your people.
👥
Culture-First Change
No framework succeeds without the culture to sustain it. Deliberate culture architecture: hiring, rituals, leadership behaviors, and the human engine that makes everything else work.
📈
Incrementally Scalable
Start with one journey, one segment, and one proof point. Scale from a contained pilot to full organizational transformation without the risk of doing too much too soon.
📊
Metrics That Matter
Replace vanity dashboards with metrics that reveal customer progress. Outcome attainment rates, time-to-value, expansion readiness, and AI confidence scores that drive real intervention.
